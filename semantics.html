Kim Ji-soo: Vocalist, Actress, and Global Icon

This article provides a brief profile and career journey of Kim Ji-soo (Jisoo) from her trainee days to becoming one of the most influential K-Pop superstars globally

1. Early Career & Visual Foundation

Kim Ji-soo was born on January 3, 1995, and began her training at YG Entertainment in 2011. Known for her classics and elegant visuals, she appeared in various advertisements and pre-debute music videos, including Epik High's MV and the drama The Producers, making her a recognizable face before officially debuting with BLACKPINK.

2. BLACKPINK's Global Success & Brand Power

Debuting in 2016, Jisoo is a crucial part of BLACKPINK's monumental success. Her unique and stable voice provides a distinct color to the group's songs. With BLACKPINK, she has broken global sales and streaming records, notably being the first K-Pop girl group to headline Coachella. Furthermore, Jisoo serves as a Global Ambassador for the luxury fashion house Dior and jewellery brand Cartier, solidifying her dominance in the high-end fashion industry.

3. Solo Expansion: Acting, Music, and BLISSOO

Jisoo has proven her capabilities as a multi-talented artist. She successfully made her leading acting debut in the drama "Snowdrop" (2021-2022), for which she won an actress award. Her solo music debut in 2023 with the song "Flower" set new sales records for a female K-Pop soloist. More recently, her collaboration "Eyes Closed" with Zayn Malik (2025) charted on the Billboard Hot 100. She now manages her solo activities under her personal label, BLISSOO.
