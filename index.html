A Day in My Life

# Embrace the blessing of today #

Morning

  1. Wake up at 4:00 AM
  2. Morning stretch
  3. Prepare and eat breakfast
  4. Shower and get ready for the day
  5. Commute to work/Start working (9:00 AM)
  6. Check and respond to emails/messages
  7. Work on main tasks/projects

Noon

  • Lunch break (12:00 PM)
  • Finish work (5:00 PM)
  • Commute back home/Run errands (e.g., grocery shopping)

Evening

  • Prepare and eat dinner
  • Relaxation/Hobbies (e.g., reading, gaming, watching a show)
  • Prepare for the next day (e.g., lay out clothes, pack bag)
  • Bedtime routine (e.g., brush teeth, skincare)
  • Go to sleep (10:00 PM)
